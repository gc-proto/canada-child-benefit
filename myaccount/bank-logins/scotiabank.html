With SecureKey Concierge, you can access Government of Canada online services using your online and mobile banking sign-in credentials. No online service receives information about the bank to which your sign-in credentials relate. Similarly, Scotiabank receives no information about which online service you are accessing.

The only information Scotiabank provides to SecureKey Concierge is a randomly generated number that is created the first time you sign into SecureKey Concierge. That number is matched to another randomly generated number that corresponds to you and which is held by the Government of Canada online service. Each subsequent time you sign in using SecureKey Concierge, your Scotiabank number is matched to your Government of Canada number, allowing you to access the Government of Canada online service. No personally identifying information or banking details are ever shared between Scotiabank and the Government of Canada online service.

SecureKey Concierge is a commercial service operating independently of Scotiabank, other financial and commercial institutions, and government.
